🎓 About TCEA

Our mission, our pillars, our people

Est. 1980

Technology in service of every learner.

TCEA (Texas Computer Education Association) is a member-based organization devoted to advancing technology in teaching and learning. We want to dismantle and eliminate barriers in education and make learning possible for every student — through advocacy, professional learning, and community.
